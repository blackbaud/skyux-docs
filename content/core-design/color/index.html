As a general rule, {{ stache.config.product_name_short }} uses color to convey meaning and not as pure decoration. You should rarely apply colors directly to elements because components incorporate colors as necessary. If a need for color arises outside of a component, you likely need for to create a component for that scenario.

Background, border, and text colors are derived from base colors through tinting and shading. Colors for information visualizations can be found in the palette component

.
For text, ensure that the contrast in color between the text and the background is at least 4.5:1 to meet Web Content Accessibility Guidelines 2.0 level AA.
